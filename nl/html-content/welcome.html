Aan de slag

Stap 1
Open the Scratch Addons popup

You may need to open the Extensions list by clicking the puzzle icon before finding it.

Stap 2
Druk op het tandwielpictogram om naar de Instellingspagina te gaan

Dit zal de Instellingspagina openen waar je kunt aanpassen hoe de extensie werkt.

Stap 3
Scroll door de addonlijst en schakel degenen in die je wilt

You can also configure each of the addons on the bottom section of each addon.

Pin the extension

In some browsers, you should take your time to pin the extension.

Open the Extensions list by clicking the puzzle icon and find Scratch Addons. Then, you can pin it by pressing the icon, or right clicking the extension and pin it through the menu.
